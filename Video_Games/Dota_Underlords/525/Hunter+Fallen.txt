====== Hunter+Fallen ======
Created Tuesday 06 October 2020

{{.\pasted_image.png?width=500}}
Pick up ★★ of each on your way to level 9, may want to stop @ 7 to get ★★★ Terrorblade

=== Build Variations ===
Aim for 6 Fallen Hunter, But we can also mix Hunter + Heartless + Vigilant with anything Tanky (like the brutes)

=== Strength/Weakness ===
* Great Vs All Builds with correct positioning adjustments (especially vs assassins)
* Can lose to Shamans Summoner, Assassins, Brutes when there is overwhelming numbers of ★★★ on the enemy side.

=== Key Unit/Items: ===
* ★★★ Terrorblade, ★ Wraith King (start the 6 fallen)
* Barricade
* Desolator on Medusa
* Enthrall Anessix!
