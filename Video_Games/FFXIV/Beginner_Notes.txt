====== Beginner Notes ======
Created Friday 15 January 2021

* Turn down Battle Effects
	* Character Config -> Character -> Battle Effects Settings -> Show Limited
* Limsa Lominsa is probably the best, but go with your heart.
	* Tree huggers
	* Pirates
	* Desert
* [[https://www.youtube.com/user/MTQcapture|MTQcapture]]
* Controller Support is great. You can end-game raid with a controller!
* Do the MSQ above all else!
* [[https://ffxiv.consolegameswiki.com/wiki/Content_Unlock|Content Unlock]] - Do the blue '+' quests!
* Level 30 - Chocobo!
* Speedy Leveling can be done in Deep Dungeons & Daily Duty Finder
	* The Palace of the Dead from level 17 (floors 51-60)
	* Heaven on High from level 61 (floors 21-30)
* If you're not with a friend, utilize the Novice Network
* Item Level > stats
* Classes are crazy balanced in this game - Pick one that sings to you
	* [[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k_vH-1WZQ3A|Larryzaur's Which Job to play video]]
