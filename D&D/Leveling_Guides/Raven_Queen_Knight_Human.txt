====== Raven Queen Knight Human ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 15 (9) DEX 8 (0) CON 14 (7) INT 8 (0) WIS 10 (3) CHA 15 (9)

**Choosing the Human race gives you:**
* +1 Str, +1 Cha
* **Feat: Pole Arm Master**
* Languages: Common and Dwarven

**Choose the Knight Background:**
* Proficiency with History and Persuasion
* Languages: Elven
* **Feature: Retainer**

**Take six (6) levels of Paladin**
Paladin Level 1:

* Proficiency with all armor, shields and weapons
* Proficiency with Wisdom and Charisma saving throws
* Divine Sense
* Lay on Hands

* Choose the Fighting Style: Dueling
* Divine Smite
* Choose the spells: //Bless, Command, Cure Wounds, Detect Magic//

* Choose the **Vengeance** subclass
	* Gain the spells: //Bane, Hunter's Mark//
	* Gain the Channel Divinity options: Abjure Enemy, Vow of Enmity
Paladin Level 4:
* Choose the spell: //Shield of Faith//
* **Feat: Warcaster**
Paladin Level 5:
* Extra Attack
* Exchange the spell //Shield of Faith// for //Find Steed//
* Gain the spells: //Hold Person, Misty Step//
Paladin Level 6:
* Aura of Protection
* Choose the spell: //Lesser Restoration//
