====== Forge Cleric Dwarf ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 15 (9) DEX 11 (3) CON 12 (4) INT 8 (0) WIS 15 (9) CHA 10 (2)

**Choosing the Hill Dwarf race gives you:**
* Con +2, Wis +1
* Darkvision 60ft
* Dwarven Resilience
* Proficiency with Battleaxe, Handaxe, Light Hammer, and Warhammer
* Proficiency with Mason’s Tools
* Stonecunning
* Dwarven Toughness
* Languages: Common and Dwarvish

**Choose the Guild Artisan Background:**
* Proficiency with Insight and Persuasion
* Proficiency with Leatherworker’s Tools
* Languages: Orcish
* **Feature: Guild Membership**

**Take twenty (20) levels of Cleric**
Cleric Level 1:
* Proficiency with History and Religion
* Proficiency with Medium Armor, Shields, and Simple Weapons
* Proficiency with Wisdom and Charisma saving throws
* Choose the cantrips: //Thaumaturgy, Toll the Dead, Guidance//
* Choose the spells: //Bless, Command, Detect Magic, Healing Word//
* Gain the spells: //Identify, Searing Smite//
* Choose the **Forge Domain** subclass:
	* Proficiency with Heavy Armor and Smith’s Tools
	* Blessing of the Forge
Cleric Level 2:
* Channel Divinity (1/rest)
* Artisan’s Blessing
* Turn Undead
* Choose the spell //Guiding Bolt//
Cleric Level 3:
* Choose the spell //Spiritual Weapon//
* Exchange the spell //Guiding Bolt// for //Hold Person//
* Gain the spells: //Heat Metal, Magic Weapon//
Cleric Level 4:
* **ASI: Wisdom +2 (18)**
* Choose the spells: //Blindness/Deafness, Aid//
Cleric Level 5:
* Destroy Undead (CR ½)
* Choose the spell //Revivify//
* Exchange the spell //Blindness/Deafness// for //Spirit Guardians//
* Gain the spells: //Elemental Weapon, Protection from Energy//
Cleric Level 6:
* Channel Divinity (2/rest)
* Forge Domain feature: Soul of the Forge
* Choose the spell //Dispel Magic//
Cleric Level 7:
* Choose the spell //Guardian of Faith//
* Gain the spells: //Fabricate, Wall of Fire//
Cleric Level 8:
* **Feat: War Caster**
* Destroy Undead (CR 1)
* Forge Domain feature: Divine Strike
* Choose the spell //Banishment//
Cleric Level 9:
* Choose the spell //Mass Cure Wounds//
* Exchange the spells: //Command// for //Geas//, //Revivify// for //Raise Dead//
* Gain the spells: //Animate Objects, Creation//
Cleric Level 10:
* Divine Intervention
* Choose the spell //Flame Strike//
Cleric Level 11:
* Destroy Undead (CR 2)
* Choose the spell //Heroes’ Feast//
Cleric Level 12:
* **ASI: Wisdom +2 (20)**
* Choose the spells: //Heal, True Seeing//
Cleric Level 13:
* Choose the spell //Plane Shift//
Cleric Level 14:
* Destroy Undead (CR 3)
* Choose the spell //Resurrection//
Cleric Level 15:
* Choose the spell //Antimagic Field//
Cleric Level 16:
* **Feat: Heavy Armor Master (Str +1 [16])**
* Choose the spell //Fire Storm//
Cleric Level 17:
* Destroy Undead (CR 4)
* Forge Domain feature: Saint of Forge and Fire
* Choose the spell //Mass Heal//
* Exchange the spell //Resurrection// for //True Resurrection//
Cleric Level 18:
* Channel Divinity (3/rest)
* Choose the spell //Revivify//
Cleric Level 19:
* **ASI: Strength +2 (18)**
* Choose the spell //Create Undead//
Cleric Level 20:
* Divine Intervention Improvement
* Choose the spell //Regenerate//
