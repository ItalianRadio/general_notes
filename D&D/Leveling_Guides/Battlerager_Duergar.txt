====== Battlerager Duergar ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 15 (9) DEX 14 (7) CON 14 (7) INT 8 (0) WIS 12 (4) CHA 8 (0)

**Choosing the Dwarven Duergar race gives you:**
* +2 Con, +1 Str
* Darkvision 120ft
* Dwarven Resilience
* Duergar Resilience
* Proficiency with Battleaxes, Handaxes, Light Hammers, and Warhammers
* Proficiency with Smith’s Tools
* Stonecunning
* Sunlight Sensitivity
* Languages: Common and Dwarvish

**Choose the Guild Artisan Background:**
* Proficiency with Athletics and Insight
* Proficiency with Leatherworker’s Tools
* Languages: Giant
* Feature: Guild Membership

**Take five (5) levels of Barbarian**
Barbarian Level 1:
* Proficiency with Intimidation and Perception
* Proficiency with medium armor, shields, and all weapons
* Proficiency with Strength and Constitution saving throws
* Rage (+2, 1/day), Unarmored Defense
Barbarian Level 2:
* Reckless Attack
* Danger Sense
Barbarian Level 3:
* Choose the **Battlerager** subclass
* Equip Spiked Armor
* Battlerager Armor
* Rage (+2, 2/day)
Barbarian Level 4:
* **Feat: Grappler**
Barbarian Level 5:
* Extra Attack
* Fast Movement (+10ft)

**Take three (3) levels of Fighter**
Fighter Level 1:
* Choose the Great Weapon Fighting Fighting Style
* Second Wind
Fighter Level 2:
* Action Surge
Fighter Level 3:
* Choose the **Brute** subclass
* Brute Force (+1d4)

**Take twelve (12) levels of Barbarian**
Barbarian Level 6:
* Battlerager Feature: Reckless Abandon
* Rage (+2, 4/day)
Barbarian Level 7:
* Feral Instinct
Barbarian Level 8:
* **ASI: +2 Strength (18)**
Barbarian Level 9:
* Brutal Critical (+1 die)
* Rage (+3, 4/day)
Barbarian Level 10:
* Battlerager Feature: Battlerager Charge
Barbarian Level 11:
* Relentless Rage
Barbarian Level 12:
* **ASI: +2 Strength (20)**
* Rage (+3, 5/day)
Barbarian Level 13:
* Brutal Critical (+2 die)
Barbarian Level 14:
* Battlerager Feature: Spiked Retribution
Barbarian Level 15:
* Persistent Rage
Barbarian Level 16:
* **ASI: +2 Constitution (18)**
* Rage (+4, 5/day)
Barbarian Level 17:
* Brutal Critical (+3 die)
* Rage (+4, 6/day)
