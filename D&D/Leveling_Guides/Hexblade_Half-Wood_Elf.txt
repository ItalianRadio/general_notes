====== Hexblade Half-Wood Elf ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 13 (5) DEX 10 (2) CON 13 (5) INT 10 (2) WIS 12 (4) CHA 15 (9)

**Choosing the Half-Elf race, Wood Elf variant gives you:**
* +2 Cha, +1 Str, +1 Con
* Darkvision 60ft
* Fey Ancestry
* Fleet of Foot (35 Speed)
* Languages: Common, Elvish, One Extra

**Choose the Double Agent (Noble Variant) Background**
* Proficiency with Deception and Insight
* Proficiency with Disguise Kits and Calligrapher’s Supplies
* Feature: Look it up? Not in this guide for some reason

**Take two (2) levels of Fighter**
Fighter Level 1:
* Dueling Fighting Style, Second Wind
* Proficiency with Perception and Athletics
* Proficiency with all weapons, all armor, and shields
* Proficiency with Strength and Constitution saving throws
Fighter Level 2:
* Action Surge

**Take fourteen (14) levels of Warlock**
Warlock Level 1:
* **Hexblade** Patron: 
	* Hexblade’s Curse
	* Hex Warrior
* Cantrips: //Eldritch Blast, Mage Hand//
* 1st Level Spells: //Shield, Wrathful Smite//
	* Note: You can’t yet use your pact weapon as a focus, so no spells + shield, use a versatile weapon right now.
Warlock Level 2:
* Invocations: Devil’s Sight, Grasp of Hadar
* 1st Level Spell: //Hellish Rebuke//
Warlock Level 3:
* Invocation: Replace Grasp of Hadar with Improved Pact Weapon
	* Note: Your pact weapon is now a spell focus, pick up a shield
* Pact Boon: Pact of the Blade
* 2nd Level Spell: //Invisibility//
Warlock Level 4:
* **Feat: Elven Accuracy (+1 Cha)**
* Cantrip: Prestidigitation
* 2nd Level Spell: //Misty Step//
Warlock Level 5:
* Invocation: Thirsting Blade
* 3rd Level Spell: //Counterspell//
* Replace //Misty Step// with //Fly//
Warlock Level 6:
* Accursed Specter
* Invocation: Replace Devil’s Sight with Eldritch Smite
* 3rd Level Spell: //Dispel Magic//
Warlock Level 7:
* Invocation: Relentless Hex
* 4th Level Spell: //Staggering Smite//
Warlock Level 8:
* **ASI: +2 Charisma**
* 4th Level Spell: //Dimension Door//
Warlock Level 9:
* Invocation: Maddening Hex
* 5th Level Spell: //Far Step//
Warlock Level 10:
* Armor of Hexes
* Cantrip: //True Strike//
Warlock Level 11:
* Mystic Arcanum (6th level): //Soul Cage//
* New Spell: Infernal //Calling//
Warlock Level 12:
* **ASI: +2 Constitution**
* Invocation: Lifedrinker
Warlock Level 13:
* Mystic Arcanum (7th level): //Crown of Stars//
* New Spell: //Scrying//
Warlock Level 14:
* Master of Hexes

**Take one (1) level of Fighter**
Fighter Level 3:
* **Battlemaster**: Combat Superiority, Student of War
* Maneuvers: Disarming Attack, Feinting Attack, Riposte

**Take three (3) levels of Warlock**
Warlock Level 15:
* Invocation: Tomb of Levistus
* Mystic Arcanum (8th level): //Demiplane//
* New Spell: //Contact Other Plane//
Warlock Level 16:
* **Feat: Shield Master**
Warlock Level 17:
* Mystic Arcanum (9th level): //Foresight//
* New Spell: //Dream//
