====== Swift Quiver Lore Bard Tiefling ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

Winged Demon Bard
**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 8 (0) DEX 15 (9) CON 12 (4) INT 12 (4) WIS 11 (3) CHA 14 (7)

**Choosing the Winged Dispater Tiefling race gives you:**
* Cha +2, Dex +1
* Darkvision 60ft
* Resistance to Fire Damage
* 30ft Flying Speed (No heavy armor)
* Languages: Common and Infernal

**Choose the Entertainer Background:**
* Proficiency with Acrobatics and Performance
* Proficiency with Disguise Kits and a Musical Instrument
* **Feature: By Popular Demand**

**Take one (1) level of Bard**
Bard Level 1:
* Proficiency with Arcana, Perception, and Persuasion
* Proficiency with Light Armor, Simple Weapons, Hand Crossbows, Longswords, Rapiers, and Shortswords
* Proficiency with Dexterity and Charisma saving throws
* Proficiency with Three Musical Instruments
* Choose the cantrips: //Prestidigitation, Vicious Mockery//
* Choose the spells: //Cure Wounds, Detect Magic, Faerie Fire, Tasha’s Hideous Laughter//
* Bardic Inspiration (d6)

**Take two (2) levels of Fighter**
Fighter Level 1:
* Proficiency with Medium Armor, Shields, and All Weapons
* Choose the Fighting Style: Archery
* Second Wind
Fighter Level 2:
* Action Surge

**Take seventeen (17) levels of Bard**
Bard Level 2:
* Jack of All Trades
* Song of Rest (d6)
* Choose the spell //Thunderwave//
Bard Level 3:
* Choose the **College of Lore** subclass:
	* Proficiency with History, Investigation, and Deception
	* Cutting Words
* Expertise with Perception and Persuasion
* Choose the spell //Blindness/Deafness//
Bard Level 4:
* **ASI: Charisma +2 (18)**
* Choose the cantrip //Dancing Lights//
* Choose the spell //Heat Metal//
Bard Level 5:
* Bardic Inspiration (d8)
* Font of Inspiration
* Choose the spell //Hypnotic Pattern//
Bard Level 6:
* Countercharm
* College of Lore feature: Additional Magical Secrets spells:
	* //Counterspell,// //Revivify//
* Choose the spell //Dispel Magic//
Bard Level 7:
* Choose the spell //Polymorph//
Bard Level 8:
* **ASI: Charisma +2 (20)**
* Choose the spell //Dimension Door//
Bard Level 9:
* Song of Rest (d8)
* Choose the spell //Hold Monster//
* Exchange the spell //Cure Wounds// for //Mass Cure Wounds//
Bard Level 10:
* Bardic Inspiration (d10)
* Expertise with Arcana and Deception
* Magical Secrets: 
	* //Destructive Wave, Swift Quiver//
* Choose the cantrip //Mage Hand//
Bard Level 11:
* Choose the spell //Mass Suggestion//
* Exchange the spell //Tasha’s Hideous Laughter// for //Otto’s Irresistible Dance//
Bard Level 12:
* **ASI: Dexterity +2 (18)**
Bard Level 13:
* Song of Rest  (d10)
* Choose the spell //Resurrection//
Bard Level 14:
* College of Lore feature: Peerless Skill
* Magical Secrets: 
	* //Finger of Death, Plane Shift//
Bard Level 15:
* Bardic Inspiration (d12)
* Choose the spell //Feeblemind//
Bard Level 16:
* **ASI: Dexterity +2 (20)**
Bard Level 17:
* Song of Rest (d12)
* Choose the spell //Psychic Scream//
Bard Level 18:
* Magical Secrets: 
	* //Gate, Wish//
