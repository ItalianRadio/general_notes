====== Blood Cleric Grung ======
Created Wednesday 22 July 2020

Build guide specifically built for Frugglesworth VII
Spells are wrong. Right kind of spells, but wrong levels.

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 8 (0) DEX 14 (7) CON 15 (9) INT 8 (0) WIS 15 (9) CHA 10 (2)

**Choosing the Grung race gives you:**
* Dex +2, Con +1
* 25 Walking, 25 Climbing
* Proficiency with Perception
* Amphibious
* Poison Immunity
* Poisonous Skin
* Standing Leap
* Secrete Poison
* Water Dependency
* Languages: Common and Grung

**Choose the Bodyguard Background:**
* Proficiency with Insight and Intimidation
* Languages: Naga
* **Feature: Guardian's Presence**

**Free Feat (DM Fiat)**
* Feat: Observant - Wisdom +1 (16)
