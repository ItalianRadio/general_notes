====== Oathbreaker Aasimar ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

Blackguard
**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 15 (9) DEX 10 (2) CON 12 (4) INT 10 (2) WIS 11 (3) CHA 14 (7)

**Choosing the Fallen Aasimar race gives you:**
* +1 Str, +2 Cha
* Darkvision 60ft
* Resistance to Necrotic and Radiant
* Healing Hands
* Light Cantrip
* Necrotic Shroud
* Languages: Common and Celestial

**Choose the Acolyte Background:**
* Proficiency with Insight and Religion
* Languages: Dwarven and Draconic
* **Feature: Shelter of the Faithful**

**Take twenty (20) levels of Paladin**

* Proficiency with Wisdom and Charisma saving throws
* Divine Sense
* Lay on Hands
