====== Draconic Sorcerer Yuan-ti ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 8 (0) DEX 14 (7) CON 14 (7) INT 10 (2) WIS 12 (4) CHA 14 (7)

**Choosing the Yuan-Ti race gives you:**
* Cha +2, Int +1
* Darkvision 60ft
* Gain the spells (Cha):
* Poison Spray
* Animal Friendship (snakes only)
* Suggestion (Level 3)
* Magic Resistance
* Poison Immunity
* Languages: Common, Abyssal, and Draconic

**Choose the Pirate Background:**
* Proficiency with Athletics and Perception
* Proficiency with Navigator’s Tools and Water Vehicles
* **Feature: Bad Reputation**

**Take one (1) level of Sorcerer**
Sorcerer Level 1:
* Proficiency with Arcana and Deception
* Proficiency with Daggers, Darts, Slings, Quarterstaffs, and Light Crossbows
* Proficiency with Constitution and Charisma saving throws
* Choose the cantrips: //Chill Touch, Light, Mage Hand, Minor Illusion//
* Choose the spells: //Ice Knife, Fog Cloud//
* Choose the **Draconic Bloodline** subclass
	* Choose the Dragon Ancestor: Green
	* Draconic Resilience

**Take three (3) levels of Warlock**
Warlock Level 1:
* Proficiency with Light Armor and Simple Weapons
* Choose **The Fiend** subclass
	* Dark One’s Blessing
* Choose the cantrips: //Eldritch Blast, Prestidigitation//
* Choose the spells: //Armor of Agathys, Hex//
Warlock Level 2:
* Choose the invocations:
	* Elemental Blast (HOMEBREW)
	* Agonizing Blast
* Choose the spell //Hellish Rebuke//
Warlock Level 3:
* Choose the **Pact of the Chain** Pact Boon
* Choose the **Quasit** Familiar
* Choose the spell //Invisibility//

**Take sixteen (16) levels of Sorcerer**
Sorcerer Level 2:
* Font of Magic
* Choose the spell //Shield//
Sorcerer Level 3:
* Choose the Metamagics: Quickened, Twinned
* Choose the spell //Hold Person//
Sorcerer Level 4:
* Choose the cantrip //Mold Earth//
* Choose the spell //Shatter//
* **ASI: Charisma +2 (18)**
Sorcerer Level 5:
* Choose the spell Fireball
Sorcerer Level 6:
* Draconic Bloodline feature: Elemental Affinity
* Choose the spell //Haste//
Sorcerer Level 7:
* Choose the spell //Polymorph//
Sorcerer Level 8:
* Choose the spell //Banishment//
* **ASI: Charisma +2 (20)**
Sorcerer Level 9:
* Choose the spell //Cloudkill//
Sorcerer Level 10:
* Choose the spell //Hold Monster//
* Choose the metamagic Subtle Spell
Sorcerer Level 11:
* Choose the spell //Scatter//
Sorcerer Level 12:
* **ASI: Constitution +2 (16)**
Sorcerer Level 13:
* Choose the spell Plane //Shift//
* Sorcerer Level 14:
* Draconic Bloodline feature: Dragon Wings
Sorcerer Level 15:
* Choose the spell //Dominate Monster//
Sorcerer Level 16:
* **Feat: Toughness**
Sorcerer Level 17:
* Choose the metamagic Heightened Spell
* Choose the spell //Wish//
