====== Build Murray Halfling ======
Created Monday 10 June 2019

**27 Point Buy gives you:**
STR 8 (0) DEX 12 (4) CON 10 (2) INT 14 (7) WIS 13 (5) CHA 15 (9)

**Choosing the Lightfoot Halfling race gives you**
* +2 Dex, +1 Cha
* 25ft Base walking speed
* **Halfling Luck**
* **Brave, Halfling Nimbleness, Naturally Stealthy**
* Languages: Common, Halfling

**Choose the Acolyte Background:**
* Proficiency with Insight and Religion
* Proficiency with two languages

**Take three (3) levels of Rogue**
''[01]'' Rogue Level 1:
* Proficiency with Perception, Performance, Persuasion, Stealth, Thieves’ Tools, Thieves’ Cant
* **Sneak Attack (1d6), Expertise:** Perception, Thieves’ Tools
* Proficiency with Dexterity and Intelligence saving throws
''[02]'' Rogue Level 2:
* Cunning Action
''[03]'' Rogue Level 3:
* Choose the **Mastermind** subclass
	* **Master of Intrigue:** Proficiency in disguise kit, forgery kit, one gaming set, two languages
	* **Master of Tactics**
* **Sneak Attack (2d6)**

**Take one (1) level of Cleric**
''[04]'' Cleric Level 1:
* Divine Domain: Life
	* **Bonus Proficiency: Heavy armor (-10ft Movement)**
	* **Disciple of Life**
* Spellcasting
	* **Cantrips:** //Guidance, Resistance, Spare the Dying//
	* **1st Level Spells:** //Bless, Create or Destroy Water, Cure Wounds, Sanctuary//

**Take four (4) levels of Wizard**
''[05]'' Wizard Level 1:
* Arcane Recovery
* **Spellcasting**
* **Cantrips**: //Mold Earth, Mending, Prestidigitation//
* **1st Level Spells:** //Alarm, Detect Magic, Identify, Longstrider, Tenser’s Floating Disk, Unseen Servant//
''[06]'' Wizard Level 2:
* **Arcane Tradition**
* **School of Divination**:
	* **Divination Savant, Portent**
* **1st Level Spells**: //Comprehend Languages, Jump, Protection From Evil and Good//
''[07]'' Wizard Level 3:
* **2nd Level Spells**: //Enlarge/Reduce, Invisibility, Levitate, Locate Object//
''[08]'' Wizard Level 4:
* **2nd Level Spells:** //Darkvision, Magic Weapon, See Invisibility, Spider Climb//
* **Feat: War Caster**

**Take one (1) level of Fighter**
''[09]'' Fighter Level 1:
* **Protection Fighting Style, Second Wind**
* Proficiency with simple and martial weapons, light and medium armor, and **shields**
**Take five (5) levels of Wizard**
''[10]'' Wizard Level 5:
* **3rd Level Spells:** //Catnap, Haste, Tiny Servant, Tongues//
''[11]'' Wizard Level 6:
* **3rd Level Spells:** //Clairvoyance, Fly, Leomund’s Tiny Hut, Remove Curse//
''[12]'' Wizard Level 7:
* **4th Level Spells:** //Dimension Door, Fabricate, Locate Creature, Polymorph//
''[13]'' Wizard Level 8:
* **4th Level Spells:** //Arcane Eye, Leomund’s Secret Chest, Mordenkainen’s Private Sanctum, Stoneskin//
* **Feat: Mobility (+10ft Movement!)**
* If you have 15 Strength from a magic item then go:
	* Observant (+1 Wis)
''[14]'' Wizard Level 9:
* **5th Level Spells:** //Skill Empowerment, Rary’s Telepathic Bond, Teleportation Circle//

**Take three (3) levels of Bard**
''[15]'' Bard Level 1:
* Proficiency with Medicine, one musical instrument
* **Bardic Inspiration (d6)**
* **Spellcasting**
	* **Cantrips**: //Light, Mage Hand//
	* **1st Level Spells:** //Cure Wounds, Detect Magic, Feather Fall, Identify//
''[16]'' Bard Level 2:
* **Jack of All Trades, Song of Rest (d6)**
* **1st Level Spells:** //Heroism//
''[17]'' Bard Level 3:
* Choose the **College of Glamour**
	* **Mantle of Inspiration**
* **Expertise**: Medicine, Perception
* **2nd Level Spells**: //Lesser Restoration//
* **Replace**: //Heroism// with //Enhance Ability//

**Take two (2) levels of Fighter**
''[18]'' Fighter Level 2:
* **Action Surge**
''[19]'' Fighter Level 3:
* Choose the **Battle Master** archetype
	* Student of War: Proficiency in artisan’s tools
	* **Combat Superiority**
		* **Maneuvers**: //Commander’s Strike, Maneuvering Attack, Rally//

**Take one (1) level of Cleric**
''[20]'' Cleric Level 2:
* **Channel Divinity: Preserve Life**
* **1st Level Spells:** //Purify Food and Drink//
