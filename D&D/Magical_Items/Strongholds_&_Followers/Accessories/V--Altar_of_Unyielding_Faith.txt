====== V--Altar of Unyielding Faith ======
Created Tuesday 18 June 2019

//Wondrous item, very rare, requires installation//

Install this altar in your temple and choose a row on the Servitors chart (page 31). Whenever you roll on this chart, you may choose the chart entry one higher than the result you rolled.
