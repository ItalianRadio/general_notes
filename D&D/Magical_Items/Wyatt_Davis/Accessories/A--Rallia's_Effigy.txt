====== A--Rallia's Effigy ======
Created Saturday 21 September 2019

//Wondrous item, artifact (requires attunement)//

While attuned all creatures under your control that are within 120 ft of you deal an additional 8 damage whenever they deal damage. (Does not apply to yourself)
