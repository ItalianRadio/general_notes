====== A--Lifewell ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Wondrous item, artifact

Glorious Glass Bottle, stopped up by thorny vines growing out of the top

As an action, you may use the bottle to heal a creature within 5ft to full health. Upon a successful use of the **Lifewell** the user ages by 10 years.
