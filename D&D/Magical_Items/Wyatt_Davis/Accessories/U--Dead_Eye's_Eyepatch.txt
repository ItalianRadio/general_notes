====== U--Dead Eye's Eyepatch ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

//Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)//

The eyepatch can hold **4 Charges, regaining 1d4+1 every day**
1C: gain advantage on a ranged attack roll. This bonus can only be granted before you make your roll. 

Additionally it grants proficiency in Intimidation

Finally while you are attuned to the eyepatch you have Disadvantage on all Wisdom (Perception) rolls made relying on your eyesight
