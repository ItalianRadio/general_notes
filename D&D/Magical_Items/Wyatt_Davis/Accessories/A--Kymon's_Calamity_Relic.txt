====== A--Kymon's Calamity Relic ======
Created Saturday 21 September 2019

//Wondrous item, artifact//

If you crit on a melee attack automatically casts //melf's minute meteors//. No concentration. If you crit while you still have meteors, increase your meteors to the maximum amount. You must still use a bonus action to invoke the meteors.
