====== R--Kymon's Loincloth ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

//Wondrous item, rare (requries attunement)//

A creature attuned to the loincloth only gains use of its abilities while it is not only not wearing armor, but also not wearing any clothing that would cover their stomach or thighs down to the kneecap. While you satisfy these requirements you are granted the following bonuses:
* A +1 bonus to AC
* A +1 bonus to all melee weapon attacks and damage rolls
* You gain proficiency in Intimidation, if you already have proficiency, you gain expertise.
