====== R--Rhowari Mantle ======
Created Friday 20 September 2019

//Wondrous item, rare (requries attunement)//

Expertly crafted mantle filigree of suns on the pauldrons with a fancy attachment chains.

Grant resistance to acid & poison damage
Once per day you can use an action to regain a spell slot of up to 3rd level. The mantle regains this ability every day at dawn.
