====== U--Silencer (For Wands) ======
Created Monday 24 August 2020

//Wondrous item, uncommon//

This wondrous item has 4 charges. When this cylindrical metal tube is screwed on top of a magical wand it allows the user to cast //silence// from said wand by using an action to expend 1 charge. Each morning at dawn the silencer regains 1d4 charges.
