====== V--Radnelak Cutlass ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

//Weapon (any one-handed sword), very rare (requires attunement)//

This +2 sword grants the following abilities depending on the time of day:

Gain the following ability during the daytime:
* Radnelak Cutlass deals an additional +1d8 necrotic dmg

Gain the following abilities during the nighttime:
* You appear to be a skeleton
* Gain sunlight sensitivity
* At the start of your turn gain temporary HP equal to your Charisma modifier
