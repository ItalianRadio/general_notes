====== R--Sea Seeker ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Weapon (sickle), rare

This +2 sickle grants the following ability:

**Might of the Sea.** If you make 1 attack on a turn and it is made with this weapon, the **Sea Seeker** deals 1d20 damage instead of 1d4.
