====== A--Kaloma's Fury ======
Created Saturday 23 November 2019

This magical battle fan is a +3 finesse weapon that deals 1d8 slashing damage on an attack.

This fan has 15 charges and regains 1d12+3 expended charges daily at dawn.
You can use the fan to expend the charges in the following ways:
To use an action to cast the following wind-based spells: 
1 charge: //thunderwave, warding wind//
2 charges: //gust of wind//
3 charges: //wind wall//
5 charges: //control winds, destructive wave, conjure elemental (air)//
6 charges: //investiture of wind//
7 charges: //whirlwind//
8 charges: //control weather//

Use your own spellcasting ability for these spells, otherwise use Charisma.''s''
