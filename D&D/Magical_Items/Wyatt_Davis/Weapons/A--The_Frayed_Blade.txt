====== A--The Frayed Blade ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

//Weapon (war scythe), artifact (requires attunement)//

This +3 war scythe appears to turn into mist. You can't tell where the blade ends, it just seems to vanish into nothingness.

This weapon has 6 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 each morning. It has the following abilities:
* 2C: As an attack, you may attack each creature in a 30ft line. 
* 4C: You gain Advantage on all attacks made with the **Frayed Blade** until the beginning of your next turn.
