====== R--Olman's Axe ======
Created Friday 28 June 2019

//Weapon (battleaxe), very rare (requires attunement)//

This +2 battleaxe asks you to roll a d6 on every attack, on a 6 you deal an extra 2d8 cold damage to all creatures of your choice within 10ft of the target you hit.
