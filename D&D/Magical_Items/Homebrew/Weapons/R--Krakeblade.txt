====== R--Krakeblade ======
Created Thursday 13 June 2019

//Weapon (greatsword), rare (requires attunement)//

A massive sword that is able to be wielded fluidly despite its size and weight. It has the following abilities:
* While within 15ft. of a pool of water at least 2in. deep and 5ft. in diameter it is a +2 magical weapon and glows in a blue-green color and casts dim light in this color out 10ft.
* While you are attuned to a //krakeblade// you gain a swim speed of 30ft. and can breathe underwater.
* You can cast //wall of water//. Once the //krakeblade //is used in this way, you cannot cast it again until the next day at dawn.
