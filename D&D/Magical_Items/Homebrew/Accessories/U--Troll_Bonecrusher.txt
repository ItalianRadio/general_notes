====== U--Troll Bonecrusher ======
Created Friday 14 June 2019

//Weapon (any weapon that deals bludgeoning damage), uncommon//

Once per turn when you attack someone with the bonecrusher, if you miss, you may have it lower the foe's AC by 1 for 1 hour.
