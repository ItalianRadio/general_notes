====== U--Jackal Mask ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

//Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)//

Once per short rest you may use this item to grant yourself Advantage on an attack roll.

While you are attuned to the **Jackal Mask** you have proficiency in Dexterity ST.
