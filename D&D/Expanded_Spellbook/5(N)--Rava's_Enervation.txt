====== 5(N)--Rava's Enervation ======
Created Sunday 20 October 2019

//Level:// **5**
//Casting time:// **1 Action**
//Range:// **Touch**
//Components:// **V, S**
//Duration:// **Concentration, up to 1 minute**

Imbue a willing creature you touch with the energy of enervation. As an action you imbue the creature will negative energy with a will that seeks out creatures to heal the imbued host. After imbuement the caster can use a bonus action to cause all creatures of your choice within 10 feet of the host to make a Dexterity saving throw, on a failure, the creatures take 4d8 necrotic damage and the host heals for an amount equal to the damage dealt. On a success the creature takes half damage and the host heals for an amount equal to the damage dealt.
