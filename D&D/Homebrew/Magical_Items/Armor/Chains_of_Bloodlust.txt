====== Chains of Bloodlust ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Requires Full Attunement
+3 Chainmail
**Grants Abilities: Bloodlust**

**Bloodlust**:
**Bloodlust **is activated as a bonus action, while activated you gain the following benefits:
* You have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
* When you make a melee weapon attack, you gain a +3 bonus to the damage roll.
* You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
* If you are able to cast spells, you can’t cast them or concentrate on them while **Bloodlust** is activated.

Your **Bloodlust** ends early if you are knocked unconscious or if your turn ends and you haven’t attacked a hostile creature since your last turn or taken damage since then. Otherwise, there are no time limits. You can also end your **Bloodlust** on your turn as a bonus action.

When **Bloodlust **ends, you suffer from 1 additional level of exhaustion.
