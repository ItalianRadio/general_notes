====== Golden Dragon Mask ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Requires Full Attunement
Mask

While you are wearing the Dragon Mask you have +1 all ST and are granted the **Dragonborn Breath** ability.

**Dragonborn Breath:**
As an action you breathe through the mask, damaging your foes with a dragon's breath. All enemies in a 15ft. cone must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be dealt fire damage by an amount decided by how many times you have used this ability since you last took a long rest.

1. 6d6
2. 5d6
3. 4d6
4. 3d6
5+. 2d6
