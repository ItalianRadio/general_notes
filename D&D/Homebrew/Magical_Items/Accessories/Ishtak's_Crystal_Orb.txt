====== Ishtak's Crystal Orb ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Requires Full Attunement
Crystal Ball

1/day when you know the roll an foe has made against a ST you have imposed, but before you know the result, you may add +2 to the DC of a spell. This bonus is applied to all remaining foes who have yet to make their saving throws against the same spell.
