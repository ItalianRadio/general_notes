====== Blood-Soaked Cape ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Take 15dmg 1turn, blood on melee weps, +4d6 next melee atk. After 7 uses +4d8 -1 Wis (RC5) After 12 uses +4d10 -1Wis (RC6), After 17 uses -2Con (RC7), After 18 uses you disappear into shadow and come back 1d4 days later in the cloak as a revenant (Sunlight sensitivity, -2Wis (RC8), below 1/2 HP, heal prof beginning of turn) cloak deals +4d6 again (A)
