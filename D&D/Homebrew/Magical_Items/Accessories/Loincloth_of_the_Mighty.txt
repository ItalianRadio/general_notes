====== Loincloth of the Mighty ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

While wearing ONLY the loincloth (no bags/belts, only other thing on your person allowed is weapons) +1 AC, +1 to hit & dmg w/ 2H wep, +prof to Intimidation
