====== Backbiter ======
Created Wednesday 05 June 2019

Requires Full Attunement
+6 Pike

If you roll a 1 to hit, reroll the attack against yourself! This effect only applies if the final die result is a 1. (Meaning you still reroll the one for a lucky halfling and do not attack yourself unless the subsequent roll is also a 1)
