====== Jarvis, Hangman ======
Created Friday 21 June 2019

Grimacing old man, wears a red hankercheif around his neck, padded leather clothing. Wears a sword and pistol on a toolbelt along with a toolpouch out of which you can see a working hammer sticking out.
