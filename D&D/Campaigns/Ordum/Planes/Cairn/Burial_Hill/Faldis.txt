====== Faldis ======
Created Friday 28 June 2019

Lost Survivor is an Act 1 side quest available from Faldis in Lower Crossing.

Faldis, scavenging for supplies in the Lower Crossing, has become trapped inside a house by Rift Scourges and the walking dead
