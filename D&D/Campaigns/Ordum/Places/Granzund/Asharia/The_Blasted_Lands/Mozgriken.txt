====== Mozgriken ======
Created Friday 21 June 2019

{{.\Mozgriken.jpg}}

Mozgriken were adverse to combat, and actively avoided it, even when holding an advantage. They were rendered cowardly and evasive due to the cruelty of their masters, but due to their own weakness relative to other beings in the Underdark, they had accepted illithid rule for the most part. Because of this they were trusted to carry out reconnaissance missions for the mind-flayers without a risk of them escaping
