====== Tahnlund Port ======
Created Tuesday 28 May 2019

Human

The Chain (Before they were called the chain) defended this city from the initial incursion of the neogi and illithids, gaining the trust of the baron of Garmund. The baron perished in the defense and bequeathed his barony to the chain.
