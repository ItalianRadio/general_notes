====== Warchief ======
Created Sunday 20 October 2019

With a commanding voice and charismatic presence, the warchief rallies the valiant through inspiration, motivation, and leadership, while also bringing mayhem to their foes through thrown weapons.

===== Warchief Feature Table =====
| Rank | Features                                             |
|------|------------------------------------------------------|
| 1st  | Mighty Throw, Strong-Arm                             |
| 2nd  | "Incoming!", Chest Thumper                           |
| 3rd  | "Find Their Weaknesses!", "There's Nothing to Fear!" |
| 4th  | Vicious Attack, Mighty Critical                      |
| 5th  | Battlefield Commander, Anthem of Flame               |


===== Ascendancy Features =====
As a warchief, you gain the following ascendancy features.

=== Hit Points ===
**Hit Dice:** 1d8 per warchief rank
**Hit Points at Each Rank:** 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per warchief rank

=== Proficiencies ===
**Armor:** Shields
**Weapons:** Simple and martial weapons
**Skills:** Choose one from Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, and Survival

===== Mighty Throw =====
Starting as 1st rank, you can use a bonus action to prepare yourself to throw your weapons farther and more accurately than you otherwise feel you could. Your first range increment with thrown weapons are doubled for this turn and you deal an additional weapon die of damage this turn.

===== Strong-Arm =====
Starting when you first become a warchief you gain the ability to draw a weapon as a free action whenever you throw a weapon as part of an attack.

===== "Incoming!" =====
Upon reaching 2nd rank, you can use a bonus action to shout “Incoming” to all allies that can hear you within 30 feet. You and all allies that heard you have a +10 bonus to land speed until the end of your next turn.

===== Chest Thumper =====
At 2nd rank, whenever you make a thrown weapon attack on a foe within 5 feet of you, you can make a Strength (Athletics) check with a bonus equal to your warchief rank opposed by your foe’s Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) check.
If you win the contest, your mighty throw pushes the foe a number of feet equal to 5 * your warchief rank away from you.

===== "Find Their Weaknesses!" =====
Starting at 3rd rank, you can use a bonus action to shout “Find Their Weaknesses” to an ally (not yourself) within 30 feet. If they heard you, the next time that ally makes a weapon attack, they may reroll all 1’s and 2’s on their damage dice, keeping the result of the new roll.

===== "There's Nothing to Fear!" =====
At 3rd rank, you can use a bonus action to shout “There’s Nothing to Fear” to all allies within 30 feet. You and all allies that heard you have Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing, and force damage until the end of your next turn.

===== Vicious Attack =====
Upon reaching 4th rank, whenever you use your //Mighty Throw //ability, your thrown weapon attacks deal an additional number of d8's as slashing damage equal to your warchief rank.

===== Mighty Critical =====
At 4th rank, your thrown weapon attacks deal a critical strike on a 19. If you already land critical strikes on a 19, instead deal two extra dice of weapon damage on a critical strike.

===== Battlefield Commander =====
When you reach 5th rank your command of the battlefield is absolute. You can use a shout as a free action on each of your turns.

===== Anthem of Flame =====
At 5th rank, your attacks with thrown weapons cause foes to become alight with holy flame. You deal a bonus 1d8 radiant damage on all thrown weapon attacks.
