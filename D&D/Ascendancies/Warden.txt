====== Warden ======
Created Sunday 20 October 2019

An agile and wily survivor, the warden specializes in ranged weapon combat, beast mastery, and attunement to nature.

===== Warden Feature Table =====
| Rank | Features                                      |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|
| 1st  | Animal Companion, Combat Expert, Rapid Strike |
| 2nd  | Maiming Strike, Read the Wind, Seeking Attack |
| 3rd  | Assassin's Arrow, Skirmish Navigator          |
| 4th  | Guerrilla Blow, Symbiosis, Tailwind           |
| 5th  | You Are Always Prepared, Zephyrite's Blessing |


===== Ascendancy Features =====
As a warden, you gain the following ascendancy features.

=== Hit Points ===
**Hit Dice:** 1d8 per warden rank
**Hit Points at Each Rank:** 1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per warden rank

=== Proficiencies ===
**Armor:** Medium armor, shields
**Skills:** Choose one from Acrobatics, Arcana, Insight, Perception, and Religion

==== Preparations ====
A warden focuses its mind letting it perform shots with unerring accuracy. Using 10 minutes you can focus yourself, focusing your energy, causing your attacks to have further effects. A Preparation lasts until you start a short or long rest, but you can apply a new one during a short or long rest.
Preparations only apply to ranged weapon attacks or attacks with finesse weapons.

===== Animal Companion =====
Starting at 1st rank, you obtain a hawk that you can command as an action. The hawk has the statistics from the Monster Manual. 
While it is touching you, you can communicate telepathically, but as it cannot speak you communicate more on instinct and feelings rather than through language. Additionally, while it is touching you, it does not have to make saving throws and attacks made against it will target you instead as if by magic. Finally, while it is touching you, you have Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks.
If you fall unconscious it takes to the skies to circle you. If it dies you can perform an hour long ritual to revive it.

===== Combat Expert =====
Beginning when you first become a warden you gain the ability to call your weapons to you. You gain the ability to stow or draw weapons as many times as you like on your turn as a free action.

===== Rapid Strike =====
**Preparation.** Upon choosing this path at 1st rank you learn this preparation. Expending this preparation allows you to make an attack using your bonus action.

===== Maiming Strike =====
Starting at 2nd rank, you can use an action to command your hawk to strike at a foe within 30 feet. This attack has the following bonuses:
**Your proficiency + your dexterity or wisdom modifier**
This attack deals 1d10 + your warden rank in piercing damage. If the attack hits a creature of the size Large or smaller, the creature makes a Strength saving throw. If it fails, the creature is knocked prone.

===== Read the Wind =====
At 2nd rank, whenever you make a ranged weapon attack, until the end of your next turn, your next melee weapon attack deals an additional 1d12 thunder damage as you gather the wind into your blow.

===== Seeking Attack =====
**Preparation.** Upon reaching 2nd rank you learn this preparation. Expending this preparation allows you to cause one attack that missed to hit instead.

===== Assassin's Arrow =====
**Preparation.** Upon reaching 3rd rank you learn this preparation. Expending this preparation allows you to cause a ranged weapon attack that you hit with to against a surprised creature to be a critical hit.
Once you expend this preparation, you may ready another preparation that is not already prepared.

===== Skirmish Navigator =====
Beginning at 3rd rank, whenever you make a melee attack, you may take the Disengage action as a free action.

===== Guerrilla Blow =====
**Preparation.** When you reach 4th rank you learn this preparation. You may expend this preparation upon scoring a successful melee attack. When you expend this preparation you may move up to your movement speed an then make a Dexterity (Stealth) check as a free action.

===== Symbiosis =====
At 4th rank, while you are touching your hawk your ranged attacks do not have disadvantage in melee. Additionally, when you make a ranged attack in melee, as a bonus action you may have your hawk perform a //maiming strike//.

===== Tailwind =====
Starting at 4th rank, whenever you make a ranged weapon attack and the turn after making a melee weapon attack and vice versa, your land speed increases by 10 feet and your next attack crits on a 19 or 20. This effect expires at the end of your next turn.

===== You Are Always Prepared =====
Starting at 5th rank you can prepare all your known preparations at once, expending them all separately.

===== Zephyrite's Blessing =====
At 5th rank, on your turn as a free action you can choose one creature within 30 feet. Until the start of your next turn that creature can fly. Unless you choose yourself as the target, this flying movement must end with that creature touching the ground, if you do end your turn not touching the ground, you must choose yourself with the next use of this ability.
